PARIS

Paris, the capital of France, is famously known as the City of Light and a global center for art, fashion, and culture. Its charm lies in its iconic landmarks, elegant streets, and romantic ambiance. Key highlights include: The Eiffel Tower: A symbol of Paris and one of the most visited monuments in the world. The Louvre: Home to the famous Mona Lisa, it's the world's largest art museum. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of Gothic architecture (currently under restoration). Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe: Perfect for a scenic walk and historic grandeur. Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur: A bohemian district with stunning views of the city. Paris is also renowned for its café culture, exquisite cuisine, and haute couture.
